About This Portal

What the OPD portal does and how it helps your team.

Core Capabilities

  • Secure user authentication and hashed passwords.
  • Role hierarchy: super admin, admin, and sub users.
  • Admin tools to create, activate/deactivate, and delete managed users.
  • Simple profile management and password change flows.

How Permissions Work

Super admins can manage all users except themselves. Admins can only manage users they created. Sub users can access their own account pages and assigned portal features.
