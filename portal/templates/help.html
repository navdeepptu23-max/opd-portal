Help Center

Quick answers to common questions.

I forgot my password

Contact your admin for an account reset, or if you can still log in, open your profile and use Change Password.

I cannot access admin actions

Only users with Admin or Super Admin roles can manage other users. Ask your Super Admin to update your role.

Why is my account inactive?

Your account may have been deactivated by an admin. Contact your administrator to reactivate access.
